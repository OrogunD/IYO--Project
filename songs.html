Learn to play basic songs on the Piano complete guide

I am very excited that you have decided to learn how to play the piano and you are looking for the right way to begin, well do not worry this would help you not just how to learn the piano but also introduce you to basic songs that you can begin with.


Preparation

Before you start playing the piano, you need to know to figure out what excites you. Are you ready to be committed to this journey? Do I want to go professional? or is it just a hobby, and you want to have fun. The piano is a very therapeutic instrument. Do you wish to play for relaxation and release all the pain and agony, the piano helps to do that, but whatsoever the reason may be, the main and foremost important reason anyone should learn the piano is for their satisfaction,At the end you will be delighted to have picked up the piano and letting it into your life.


Learning the lines and spaces

Back in high school I was taught that every good pianist should understand how to play from written solfa notations on the staff there are 5 horizontal lines and four spaces, they go from E-G-B-D-F. some people remember this from a saying "Every Good boy deserves fanta", it is more like a memomery trick to understand the staff and on the treble it A-C-E-G "All Cows Eat Grass" etc. This are essential tips one must understand in order to be able to play the piano fluently


Practice makes perfect

Playing the piano is a thing you have to own, learn to practice at least 30 minutes a day you don't get the best results if you practice and hour or two a week, except you are naturally gifted, even the naturally gifted on the piano still make sure they get enough practice a day. it's not a matter of how long you practice in some cases, it's a matter of how often you put in the work,make it a daily custom and I promise you would see the results.


with these few tips I believe you are ready to take on the beginner challenge, so why not go ahead and pick a song to listen to and try!!
