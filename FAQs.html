When did YouPiano start ?

YouPiano started in the year 2022,Early May by a wonderful instrumentalist named Orogun Daniel, you can contact him on his socials at the bottom of the page. He is very friendly I promise.

Is Piano the most entertaining instrument ?

Rumour has it, that the piano is one of the most enlightening instrument in the world, not only is the piano used for entertainment but has also been known as a healing instrument

Is too late to play the piano ?

It is never too late to play the piano, you can never be too old to learn something new! remember our support team is always here to help

Do I have to pay anywhere before using YouPiano ?

As of now YouPiano is a start-up and are taking first few 100 of students for free, in the future years YouPiano is looking to advance into a platform where not only would you be able to learn from the site, but also have private tutors to guide and support you on your journey.

How do I subscribe or unsubscribe to your mailing list ?

Currently YouPiano does not have stand alone mailing list but if you wish to recieve emails from us. Alternatively, you can send us an email telling us you want to updated on our recent progress and vice-versa.

Why do I keep getting an error on my browser ?

This may be because your device has either not accepted the recommended cookies or has enabled an adblocker
In order to turn off the adblocker at the top right of your device ensure the adblocker is on green this indicates that it has been disabled.

How do I speak to someone ?

You would fine a form on the contact us page or the home page, the get started button this automatically directs you to the contact us page.
