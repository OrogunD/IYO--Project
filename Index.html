METHODS

Out of hundreds of methods to learn to play piano, which is the right one for you?

Traditional method

The first is the traditional method. This one can be divided into three subsections: solfege instruction, rhythm training, and playing music with sheet music and exercises.

Simplified method

These are mostly ways that may be found on the internet, which is abundant of online piano courses and piano lessons. You've certainly seen those videos on YouTube or Dailymotion with a piano player marketing his or her method for learning to play the piano in 24 hours.

Specialized method

This is simply a method whereas you choose a genre that you are really efficient at for instance jazz, blues, hip-pop e.t.c

Classical method

The classical method is considered to be one of the most favourable method to playing the piano, classical pianist have a great flair in transposing and transcribing music.Mozart was a classical pianist
